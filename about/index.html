About Me

AI student, coder and FOSS enthusiast

Hey there! I'm Umer Khan, a first-year AI student with a passion for coding in C++, C and HTML/CSS. I'm absolutely fascinated by Linux and free software, and I love using them alongside Windows whenever possible.

I'm a hands-on learner who believes the best way to understand something is to build it. Whether I'm crafting a command-line app, creating a useful tool or troubleshooting something that decided to break, I genuinely enjoy the puzzle-solving aspect of making things work.

What really excites me? Bioinformatics, animal welfare and finding ways to use technology for positive change. I'm a cat person who believes in writing clean, readable code and loves collaborating on projects that actually make a difference.

My goal is to grow as a developer and eventually give back to the open source community that has taught me so much. I want to help others discover just how amazing free software can be.

While I'm naturally more introverted, I absolutely love connecting with fellow developers and creators who share similar passions. When I'm not buried in code, you'll likely find me rocking out to my favorite music, sipping chai or getting lost in sci-fi and superhero movies (Batman will always be my favorite!).

Got an interesting project in mind or just want to have a friendly chat about tech, cats or the latest Batman movie? I'd love to hear from you! Drop me a line at hello@umerkniazi.dev.
