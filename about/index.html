About Me

AI student, coder and FOSS enthusiast

Hi, I’m Umer Khan. I’m a first-year AI student who loves working with C++, C and HTML/CSS. I’m a big fan of Linux and free software and I use them whenever I can alongside Windows.

I learn best by doing. Whether I’m writing a command-line app, building a small tool or fixing something that broke, I enjoy figuring things out and making them work.

I’m curious about bioinformatics, animal welfare and using technology for good. I like cats, care about clean and readable code and enjoy teaming up on projects that have a purpose.

As I learn more, I hope to give back to the open source community and help others discover the power of free software.

I’m an introvert but I’m always open to working with like-minded people who share my interests. When I’m not coding, you’ll probably find me listening to rock music, drinking chai or watching sci-fi and superhero movies, especially anything with Batman.

If you’re working on something cool or just want to chat, feel free to reach out.
